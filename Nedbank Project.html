Targeted Advertising Optimization for Retail

Data-Driven Strategy to Increase Sales Through Customer Segmentation

July 2025
Excel, PowerPoint
Retail Analytics

Project Overview

Spearheaded analysis of customer demographics, product preferences, and engagement channels for a retail client to optimise ad spending. Transformed raw purchase history data into actionable segmentation strategies, driving targeted campaigns that aligned high-potential products with underutilised customer segments.

Key Objectives

  • Identify High-Value Customer Segments

    Analyse age/gender distribution and purchasing patterns

  • Optimize Product Focus

    Determine top-performing products to prioritise in campaigns

  • Channel Efficiency Improvement

    Map contact methods to age groups to boost engagement

  • Develop Data-Driven Advertising Framework

    Create segmentation rules for personalised ad targeting

Analysis & Strategy Development

Problem Statement

A retail company needs to increase sales through data-driven, targeted advertising using customer purchase history and demographic data.

Methodology

Using Excel, I performed:

  • Data Cleaning & Validation: Standardised datasets and removed inconsistencies
  • Product Performance Analysis: Evaluated sales distribution across product lines
  • Customer Segmentation: Analyzed age and gender distributions
  • Channel Preference Mapping: Correlated communication methods with customer age groups

Key Insights & Recommendations

Product Performance Analysis

Findings:
  • UT: 19 customers (38% share)
  • PROVIDENT: 15 customers (30% share)
  • RA: 13 customers (26% share)
  • LA: 3 customers (6% share)
Recommendations:
  • Allocate 70% of ad budget to UT & PROVIDENT
  • Reevaluate LA product positioning and pricing
  • Create bundled offers with RA and top performers

Customer Demographic Analysis

Findings:
  • Female customers: 25 (Avg age: 46)
  • Male customers: 25 (Avg age: 37)
Recommendations:
  • Develop age-specific messaging strategies
  • Create gender-targeted campaign variants
  • Personalize offers based on demographic profiles

Communication Channel Analysis

Findings:
  • Email: 23 customers (Avg age: 39)
  • Cell+Email: 9 customers (Avg age: 36)
  • Cell: 5 customers (Avg age: 59)
  • No contact: 13 customers (Avg age: 44)
Recommendations:
  • Prioritize email for core campaigns
  • Implement SMS+email for under-40 segment
  • Develop phone-based outreach for 55+ demographic
  • Create contact capture program for "None" segment

Strategic Implementation Plan

Product Portfolio Optimisation

Key Actions:

  • Budget reallocation: Shift 60% of marketing budget to UT9 and PROVIDENT product lines
  • Performance-based resource distribution: Allocate resources using 60/30/10 rule:
    • 60% to top performers (UT/PROVIDENT)
    • 30% to growth potential (RA)
    • 10% to experimental (LA)
  • Bundling strategy: Develop "Buy UT-119, get RA at 20% off" promotional bundles

Expected Outcomes:

  • 25-30% increase in ROI for top-performing products
  • 15% uplift in cross-selling revenue through bundling

Segmented Campaign Development

Key Actions:

  • Demographic-specific messaging:
    • Males (30-45): Tech-focused, convenience-oriented content
    • Females (45-55): Value-driven, family-oriented messaging
  • Channel optimisation:
    • Email for core messaging (all segments)
    • SMS + email for flash sales (under 40 segment)
    • Direct mail/phone for 55+ demographic
  • Personalisation engine: Implement dynamic content based on purchase history

Expected Outcomes:

  • 40% higher engagement through personalised content
  • 30% increase in conversion rates for targeted segments

Contact Information Enhancement

Key Actions:

  • In-store incentive program: Offer 10% discount for contact information sharing
  • Digital onboarding: Implement QR code-based contact collection at POS
  • Re-engagement campaign: "We miss you" email sequence with 15% discount
  • Data validation process: Quarterly contact information audits
  • Compliance framework: Ensure GDPR/POPIA compliance in data collection

Expected Outcomes:

  • 80% contact information capture rate within 6 months
  • 40% conversion of "None" segment to engaged customers
  • Comprehensive customer database for future campaigns

Performance Tracking Framework

Key Actions:

  • KPI dashboard development:
    • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
    • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
    • Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
    • Channel-specific conversion rates
  • Testing methodology: Implement A/B testing protocol for all campaigns
  • Attribution modeling: Multi-touch attribution analysis
  • Reporting cadence: Weekly performance reviews, quarterly strategy refinements
  • Competitive benchmarking: Industry-specific performance comparisons

Expected Outcomes:

  • Data-driven decision making across marketing functions
  • 20% improvement in marketing efficiency YoY
  • Continuous optimization of campaign performance
  • Clear ROI measurement for all marketing activities
